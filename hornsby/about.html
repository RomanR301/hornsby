About Richard Hornsby

Richard Hornsby is a third generation Central Floridian and a second generation criminal defense lawyer.

He obtained his law degree from the University of Florida and also holds a master’s degree in Criminal Justice from the University of Central Florida and a master’s degree in Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences, with a concentration in Forensic Science, from the University of Florida.

He is Board Certified in Criminal Trial Law by the Florida Bar and Board Certified in Criminal Trial Advocacy by the National Board of Trial Advocacy.

He serves as the Legal Analyst for the Central Florida NBC affiliate, WESH 2 News, and is frequently called upon to provide legal commentary for the Orlando Sentinel.

In 2007 he was voted Best of the Bar in Criminal Defense by the Orlando Business Journal and has been recognized by Super Lawyers Magazine as a Rising Star in criminal defense from 2012-2015 and a Super Lawyer in criminal defense from 2016-2020.
