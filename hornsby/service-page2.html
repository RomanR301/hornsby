Florida Bar Rules Regarding Past Results

The Florida Bar governs the dissemination of past results to protect potential clients from obtaining an unjustified expectation about the results a lawyer can achieve. As a result, all information concerning past results must be factually verifiable and not misleading, so please understand:

  1. The information has not been reviewed or approved by The Florida Bar.
  2. The facts and circumstances of your case may differ from the cases for which results are provided.
  3. The results of all cases handled by criminal lawyer Richard Hornsby are not provided.
  4. The results are not necessarily representative of results obtained by criminal lawyer Richard Hornsby or of the experience of other clients.
  5. Every case is different, and each client's case must be evaluated and handled on its own merits.
